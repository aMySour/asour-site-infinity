Why does this exist?

Infinity is meant to make websites feel less like a collection of pages and more like a place you visit and explore.

It also allows for much more dynamic and interconnected content. You could have a natural flow from page to page by putting relevant ones close together. Or you could have a page that's only accessible by going through a secret passage in another page.

Overall, Infinity isn't meant to replace traditional websites, but rather to be a new fun and unique kind of site.
